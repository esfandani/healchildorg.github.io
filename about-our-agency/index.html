About our Organization

  • Home
  • About our Organization

The Heal Child Skin Disease Foundation is a non-profit organization, seeking to directly help those affected by severe skin diseases and more specifically Epidermolysis Bullosa (EB). At Heal, our goal is to raise funding to sponsor children affected by such diseases. 100% of the donations we receive are allocated towards treatment of our patients.

Currently, Heal sponsors over 400 children who suffer from EB. These children are coming from low income families who cannot support their child sufficiently. With the help of our donors, Heal has managed to provide clinical services, surgeries, medical supplies, etc. for our patients. However, there is a constant need to provide our patients with the expenses required for their treatment.

BOARD MEMBERS

Hamid Adib

Dr. Hamid Adib has a diverse background that includes experience with both national and international philanthropic organizations, scientific research, development in the fields of engineering and medicine, academics, and real estate development. He is an enthusiast of the world’s finest and most unique rugs, artwork, and antiquities. An active participant in the community, Dr. Adib is a board member of the Image Reborn Foundation, the Utah Film Center, the Hope Clinic, the Salt Lake Community College (SLCC) Interior Design and Fashion Committee, and a member of the Westminster College Board of Trustees. Dr. Adib is Chairman of the Board for the Heal Child Skin Disease Foundation.

Hale Yarmohammadi

Dr. Hale Yarmohammadi graduated with a Medical Degree from Shiraz University of Medical Science in Iran. She then attended Yale University where she received her Master of Public Health in Chronic Diseases. Dr. Yarmohammadi completed her residency in Internal Medicine following two years of clinical allergy fellowship training in Allergy and Immunology at the Mount Sinai Medical Center in New York. Dr. Yarmohammadi is a Fellow of the American Academy of Allergy, Asthma and Immunology and a member of the American College of Allergy, Asthma and Immunology. She is also a member of the American College of Physicians as well as the American Medical Association.

HAMED ADIB

Dr. Hamed Adib serves as Board Member and Counsel to Heal Child Skin Disease Foundation and is responsible for managing all of the foundation's legal needs and developing creative solutions to support the achievement of the foundation's objectives to serve children suffering from severe skin diseases.

Alireza Khodaverdian

Dr. Reza Khodaverdian is board certified by the American Board of Surgery and board eligible for the American Board of Thoracic Surgery. Dr. Khodaverdian has a special interest in surgical treatment options for patients with heart failure and arrhythmias. Dr. Khodaverdian earned his medical degree from Tehran School of Medical Sciences in Tehran, Iran. He completed general surgery residencies at the University of Connecticut in Farmington, CT and at the Hospital of St. Raphael in New Haven, CT. He completed a cardiothoracic surgery fellowship at the University of Utah in Salt Lake City.
